Importance of Cows

According to Indologist, Kamadhenu or Kamaduh is the generic name of the sacred cow, who is regarded as the source of all prosperity in Hinduism. Kamadhenu is regarded as a form of Devi and is closely related to the fertile Mother Earth (Prithvi), who is often described as a cow in Sanskrit. The sacred cow denotes "purity and non-erotic fertility, sacrificing and motherly nature, sustenance of human life". Kamadhenu describes as a "popular and enduring image in Indian art".
